# README: 2_data_collection/

---

## Purpose

This folder covers Phases 2 and 3 of the project: getting annotators set up and collecting their Q&As. Once annotators are qualified, they receive their company assignments and get to work. Everything here describes how that process is structured — which documents they receive, how companies are assigned, what they produce, and how they're communicated with throughout.

---

## Files

**2_data_collection.md**
The internal project document. Covers sector and company selection, the annotator assignment logic (with the full assignment table), workflow, expected data output schema with a sample record, and the data collection budget with a full breakdown of how annotator pay is calculated. Intended for the project lead / Surge team.

**2.1_worker_facing_instructions.md**
The document sent directly to annotators at the start of the project. Covers how to access and download assigned filings from SEC EDGAR, how to write Category A / B / C Q&As with correct Supporting Facts sections, quality standards, timeline and pay expectations, and Task 2 (peer review) instructions. Ready to send alongside the annotator's assignment CSV.

**2.2_reviewer_facing_all_companies.csv**
The full list of 40 candidate companies across all sectors, with filing type, filing date, and SEC search link for each. Distributed to annotators as a reference when writing Category C (cross-document) questions that draw from companies beyond their 3 assigned ones.

**2.3_email_reminders.md**
Draft reminder emails for the annotation window. One email goes out at the midpoint (Day 3, when 3 days remain) and one on the final day (Day 5), both sent only to annotators who have not yet submitted. Ready to send as-is with date and name fields filled in.
