# README: 1_annotator_qualification/

---

## Purpose

This folder covers Phase 1 of the project: finding and vetting expert annotators. Before anyone writes a single Q&A, we need to make sure our annotators actually know their way around a 10-K. Everything in this folder is about that process.

---

## Files

**1_qualification_project.md**
The internal project document. Describes who we're looking for (candidate profiles, sourcing strategy), how the qualification task works, the three evaluation questions candidates are asked to rate, the scoring and selection criteria, and the qualification budget. Intended for the project lead / Surge team.

**1.1_qual_candidate_instructions.md**
The document sent directly to candidates. Contains the task instructions, rating scale, and the three pre-written Q&As for them to evaluate. Ready to send as-is — no internal notes or expected answers included.
